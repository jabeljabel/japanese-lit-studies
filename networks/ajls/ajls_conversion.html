Presentations vs. Proceedings Publications by Year
Stacked bars show published (matched to PAJLS volume) vs. unpublished presentations. Line shows conversion rate.
Methodology: A presentation is "matched" if the presenter appears as an author in the PAJLS proceedings volume corresponding to that conference year (PAJLS vol N = year 1998+N). Name matching uses normalized comparison (stripping middle initials, CJK characters) with last-name + first-initial fallback. Years without a corresponding PAJLS volume (2023–2025, plus PAJLS12=2010 missing from the contributors index) show as "No Volume" and are excluded from the overall conversion rate.

Matched Title Pairs
Examples of presentation titles and their corresponding proceedings articles, sorted by similarity.
About title similarity: Titles frequently change between conference presentation and proceedings publication. Jaccard similarity measures word overlap (excluding stopwords). Scores above 0.5 generally indicate the same paper; below 0.2 suggests the presenter wrote a substantially different article for the volume. Some presenters contribute entirely new papers to the proceedings that differ from their conference talk.
