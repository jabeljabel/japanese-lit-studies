SCMS Geographic Footprint

About This Visualization
Methodology

This visualization maps the geographic distribution of institutional affiliations at Society for Cinema and Media Studies (SCMS) conferences. The packed circle layout groups institutions by region, with circle size proportional to presentation count.

Institution Classification

Institutions are classified into regions (US Northeast, Midwest, South, West, Canada, Japan, UK/Europe, East Asia, Oceania, Other) based on name matching against a 200+ entry dictionary.
